Our Key Programs

Women's Empowerment

Breaking Barriers, Building Strength

For generations, Maasai women have faced deep-rooted cultural and economic challenges that limit their independence. At MWEGO, women's empowerment is not just a program—it's a movement.

  • Financial Independence Training
  • Leadership & Decision-Making
  • Legal Awareness & Rights
  • Community Engagement
Learn More

Sports Tournaments

Beyond the Game: Using Sports to Inspire and Educate

Through MWEGO's sports tournaments, we are using football as a powerful tool to unite communities, promote gender equality, and educate young people on critical social issues.

  • Mixed-gender matches
  • Social issues awareness
  • Leadership development
  • Community unity
Learn More

Feeding Program

A Meal Today, a Brighter Future Tomorrow

MWEGO's Feeding Program ensures that no child has to choose between hunger and education. By providing nutritious meals in schools and communities, we are giving children the strength to learn, grow, and build a better future.

  • School-Based Meals
  • Community Kitchens
  • Nutritional Education
  • Food Security Projects
