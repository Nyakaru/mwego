Empowering Maasai Women

MWEGO is committed to improving the lives of Maasai Mara residents through women's empowerment, sports development, and community feeding programs. Together, we're building a community where women and children are valued, empowered, and thrive.

Latest Causes

At MWEGO, we have various ongoing initiatives and projects that you can support. Learn about our current programs and how you can make a difference in the lives of Maasai women and girls.

Women's Empowerment

Empowering Maasai women through financial training, leadership, legal awareness, and community engagement.

Sports Tournaments

Uniting communities and promoting gender equality through football and other sports activities.

Feeding Program

Providing nutritious meals to children and families, supporting education and well-being.
