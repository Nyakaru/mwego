About Mwego

Empowering Maasai Women and Communities

The Maasai Mara Women Empowerment Guide Organization (MWEGO) is a community-led non-profit organization established in 2017, based in the Maasai Mara region of Kenya. We focus on improving the livelihoods of the Maasai community, especially women and girls, through education, health services, and skills training.

Our mission is to improve the lives of Maasai Mara residents by advocating for women's rights, gender equality, and health education. We are committed to empowering women and girls through education, raising awareness of reproductive health issues, and encouraging community involvement to challenge harmful cultural practices.
